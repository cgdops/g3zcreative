Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our productized models.

Why productized packages instead of hourly rates?

Hourly billing incentivizes slow work. Productized tiers focus entirely on value, giving you a fixed cost and guaranteed delivery. You know exactly what you are paying and what business outcomes to expect.

What exactly is Programmatic SEO (pSEO)?

Instead of manually writing a single blog post at a time, we build a content generation engine. This system programmatically generates hundreds of highly-targeted, localized service pages (e.g. "Roofing Contractor in Oakville", "HVAC Repair in Miramar") that rank on search engines for high-intent queries, driving massive inbound local leads.

How does the AI Retainer work?

We partner with your team to automate repetitive manual operations. First, we map out your workflows. Then, we construct automated n8n pipelines to synchronize databases, qualify leads via AI agents, and trigger instant SMS auto-responders. The retainer covers prompt tuning, new integrations, and continuous improvements as your business adapts.

Is there a contract or can we cancel?

Retainers are month-to-month. You can cancel or pause at any time with a 15-day notice. We believe in earning your partnership every single month by delivering clear, measurable business value.
